Tanaka Guitar's History


Tanaka Guitar is established in August 2021. They are based in Jakarta but there more than 100 branches all over Indonesia. Tanaka guitar aims to provide high-quality guitars from many brands.


Tanaka Guitar was founded in august 2021 by 2 university students, Anthonny Wiradharma and Adrian Natanael. They like to playing a guitar and they got an idee to open a guitar store near their house in Jakarta.
